Welcome Mathletes!

Bend Math is about bending math, hosted by yet another mathlete. We help bend it to fit our minds, to make us understand it. We value comprehention over memorizing. We think it's beneficial to get help in math, as if you don't, you may fall behind, get confused or fail a test. Come join us for $1 a day! Sign up and make a schedule with us, we're here every weekday!

We know some families need more help than others, and that's nothing to be ashamed of here. We understand. If you would like to email me about an accommodation, please do. I will read it and get back to you as soon as possible. Possibilities include but are not limited to: a weekly free visit, more one-on-one attention, or encouraging and motivational talks.

You always pay the day of, in cash or check. We will never expect anyone to pay before the day they have scheduled, as some things may come up unexpectedly. You will be encouraged to come if you are scheduled, however if you do not want to or cannot come for a day, that is understandable.
